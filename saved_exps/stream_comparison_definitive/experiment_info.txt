6-Model Ablation Design:
------------------------------------------------------------
RAW ACC+GYRO (6 channels):
  1. single_stream      - All 6ch through single projection (baseline)
  2. dual_symmetric     - Separate projections, 50/50 split
  3. dual_asymmetric    - Separate projections, 75/25 split
  4. dual_asym_dropout  - 75/25 split + modality dropout

KALMAN FUSION (7 channels):
  5. kalman_single      - Single-stream Kalman (best single-stream)
  6. kalman_dual        - Dual-stream Kalman balanced (best dual-stream)

Research Questions:
------------------------------------------------------------
RQ1: Stream separation effect     (single vs dual_symmetric)
RQ2: Capacity allocation effect   (dual_symmetric vs dual_asymmetric)
RQ3: Modality dropout effect      (dual_asymmetric vs dual_asym_dropout)
RQ4: Dual-stream for Kalman       (kalman_single vs kalman_dual)

Statistical Analysis:
------------------------------------------------------------
- Paired t-test (matched subjects)
- Cohen's d effect size
- 95% confidence intervals
- Bonferroni correction for multiple comparisons

Metrics: F1-score (primary), Accuracy, Precision, Recall
Cross-validation: 22-fold LOSO
